Nanophotonic imaging

Mentors and collaborators: Gaurav Arya, Charles Roques-Carmes, Zin Lin, Steven G. Johnson, Marin Soljačić

Through end-to-end optimization, I design nanoscale structures to enhance traditional imaging components, such as lenses in optical imaging and scintillators in X-ray imaging.
